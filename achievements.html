Achievement Icons

A personal project of mine. These are mockups of Steam achievement icons for the Crossbell Duology of the Trails series.
They're done in the style of achievement icons for the Sky and Cold Steel games. Quirky, varied, and humorous! Occasionally serious when necessary.
Done over the course of many, many, months; the style has evolved a lot since then! (Azure is a LOT better illustrated.)
