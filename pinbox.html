Pin Box

Pin Box Ltd. is a company that releases high quality licensed merchandise for various franchises.
They specialize in enamel pins that come in collector's boxes.

Gluttonous Bass Pin

The Gluttonous Bass, AKA "Bruhfish", is a meme in the Trails fandom.
Due to fan demand, Pin Box decided to develop a pin for this beloved character, and I adapted its design into an enamel pin!
