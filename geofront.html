The Geofront

The Geofront is a group of fans dedicated to bringing unlocalized Falcom games to
Western audiences at the highest possible quality.

Geofront Logos


The original draft of the Geofront logo was designed by Addaberry, and I designed the logos above.
It's based off the Enigma from 'Trails from Zero' and 'Trails to Azure', two games the Geofront localized.
The Gluttonous Bass is considered a group mascot of sorts, and has a history with several of Geofront's members.

There's a different logo design for each game, too!

Ys vs. Trails in the Sky




I'm the graphics lead for the fan translation Ys vs. Trails in the Sky: Alternative Saga.
